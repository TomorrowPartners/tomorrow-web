Council on the Arts Funding Application

To proceed with your CFA, please use the Log In and Register buttons. 

To save time you can Clone an application if you are applying for more than 1 NYSCA funding opportunity. Click here to learn how.

Log In Register

As part of Governor Cuomo’s transformative plan to improve the state’s economic development model, a NYS Consolidated Funding Application (CFA) was created to streamline and expedite the grant application process. NYSCA has used the CFA for several years as the mechanism for Regional Economic Development Council projects to submit online applications and is pleased to now roll out the use of the CFA for all funding opportunity applications for Fiscal Year 2020 (FY20).

The Resources section provides links to each of our individual Program Guidelines for FY20 and to a revised Application Resource Guide that includes step-by-step technical instructions. We urge you to read all materials before registering with the CFA and getting started on the application.

Please note that as part of NYSCA’s ongoing efforts to expedite the application process:

  • All applications this year will be entirely entered in this CFA.
  • There is no longer a need to register or provide information on the  NYSCA website as a first step.
  • The Grants Gateway is still a required and vital step in the process for the mandatory Pre-Qualification and for contracting if an award is made to your organization.

We thank you for your interest in applying to NYSCA for a FY20 grant.

Mara Manus and the NYSCA Team
